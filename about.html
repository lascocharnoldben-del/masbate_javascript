Who We Are

Masbate is a province defined by its rolling hills, pristine sandbars, and its iconic Rodeo Festival[cite: 3]. We are dedicated to showcasing the authentic island experience, from the rugged energy of the ranch to the peaceful coastal escapes of our islands.

Whether you're looking for the thrill of the Rodeo Masbateño or the quiet beauty of the Buntod Reef Marine Sanctuary, we provide the ultimate guide to the province[cite: 1, 3].
